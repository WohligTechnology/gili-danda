Volleyball at SFA

Volleyball at SFA will be conducted under the aegis of Mumbai Suburban District Volleyball Association. For the first time an inter-school volleyball tournament will be officiated and governed by an authorized body of the sport.

The volleyball arena at the venue has one mud surface court, suited and calibrated for World-class tournaments. Every match played by participants will be captured on video and later showcased on their schools SFA profile and individual participant’s SFA profile.

All medals across the different categories under volleyball, will add to the cumulative medal tally of the school, hence impacting it’s rank.

The SFA Inter-school Volleyball tournament will be conducted in the categories mentioned in the table below.

We have a maximum draw capacity as mentioned in the below table. Entries will be accommodated basis the eligibility criteria.

  • A knockout tournament will be played across all age groups and event categories for both, Boys and Girls.
  • The matches will be a best of 3 sets of 25 points each. In case of a tie then the deciding game would be of 15 points.
  • Every player must carry his/her school ID along with age proof at all times during the tournament.
  • Participants must produce their ID cards as and when required during the tournament.
  • No player will be allowed to play his/her match if they do not have their school ID along with proper age proof.
  • The team must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to their scheduled match.
  • The team has to report 15 minutes prior to their scheduled match time at the Volleyball registration desk.
  • If any participant is found over age or with any manipulation in the documents, the entire team will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • Participants must be in appropriate uniform for the sport of Volleyball. No player will be allowed to play bare feet.
  • The Volleyball organizing committee has the right to postpone or cancel the tournament in case of unavoidable circumstances. All schools are requested to co-operate in any such case.
  • In case of any dispute the decision of the Volleyball organizing committee will be final and binding.
  • No player will be allowed to use their mobile phones on court.
