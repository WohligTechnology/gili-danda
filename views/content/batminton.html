Badminton at SFA will be conducted under the aegis of the Maharashtra Badminton Association. For the first time an inter-school badminton tournament will be officiated and governed by an authorized body of the sport. The badminton arena at the venue has three xyz courts, suited and calibrated for World-class tournaments. Every match played by participants will be captured on video and later showcased on their schools and SFA profile. All medals across the different categories under badminton, will add to the cumulative medal tally of the school, hence impacting it’s rank.

  • Tournament is open to all amateur school players from districts of MSDBA / GMBA.
  • A knockout tournament will be played across all age groups and event categories for both, Boys and Girls.
  • The matches will be best of 3 games of 15 points each up till the Pre Quarter Finals. The Quarter Finals, Semi Finals and Finals will be best of 3 games of 21 points each.
  • The tournament will be conducted as per the rules & regulations for tournaments as laid down by the BWF and BAI.
  • Each participant can participate in one age category. For example, if a player is participating in the U-11 age category, he is only eligible to play the events in that same category.
  • A player enrolled for Singles in a particular age category can also play doubles only in the same age category.
  • The participant must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match.
  • Any participant found over age or with any manipulation in the documents will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • The participant has to report 15 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match time at the Badminton registration desk.
  • If the participant fails to report 7 minutes after the match is announced the opponents will get a walkover.
  • Every player must carry his/her School ID along with age proof at all times during the tournament.
  • Participant must produce their ID cards as and when required during the tournament.
  • No player will be allowed to play his/her match if they do not have they school ID along with proper age proof.
  • Any participant found over age or with any manipulation in the documents will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • Participants must be in appropriate uniform for the sport of Badminton. Only non-marking / glass sole shoes will be permitted while playing matches.
  • No player is allowed to use their mobile phones on court.

  • If any player who cannot take part in the tournament even after giving his/her entry can be replaced with another player on a special request by the respective school at least 1 day before the match starts. This is applicable only in case of doubles.
