Swimming at SFA

Swimming at SFA will be conducted under the aegis of the Swimming Federation of India. For the first time an inter-school swimming competition will be officiated and governed by an authorized body of the sport.

The swimming facility at the venue includes a state-of-the-art Olympic size swimming pool. Every event participant played by participants will be captured on video and later showcased on their schools SFA profile and individual participant’s SFA profile.

All medals across the different categories under swimming, will add to the cumulative medal tally of the school, hence impacting it’s rank.

  • In U-10 & U-12 age groups, a swimmer can participate in a maximum of 3 individual events and in relays.
  • In U-14 age group, a swimmer can participate in a maximum of 4 individual events and in relays.
  • In U-16 age group, a swimmer can participate in a maximum of 5 individual events and in relays.
  • Every swimmer must carry his/her school ID along with age proof at all times during the competition
  • Participants must produce their ID cards as and when required during the competition.
  • No swimmer will be allowed to participate if they do not have their school ID along with proper age proof.
  • The participant must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to his/her scheduled event.
  • The participant has to report 15 minutes prior to his/her scheduled event time at the Swimming registration desk.
  • Any participant found over age or with any manipulation in the documents will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • All the participants must wear swimming caps and goggles for all the swimming events.
  • The Swimming organizing committee has the right to postpone or cancel the competition in case of unavoidable circumstances. All schools are requested to co-operate in any such case.
  • In case of any dispute the decision of the Swimming organizing committee will be final and binding.
  • No player will be allowed to use their mobile phones on court.
