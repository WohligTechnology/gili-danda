The SFA Inter-school Table Tennis tournament will be conducted in the categories mentioned in the table below.

We have a maximum draw capacity as mentioned in the below table. Student entries will be accommodated basis the eligibility criteria set out in Point 3.

  • A knockout tournament will be played across all age groups and event categories for both, Boys and Girls.
  • For U-10 and U-12, matches will be best of 5 games of 11 points each till Finals.
  • For U-15 matches will be best of 5 games of 11 points each till the Quarter finals & best of 7 games of 11 points each for the Semi Finals & Finals.
  • Each participant can participate in one age category. For Example, if a player is participating in the under 10 age category he is only eligible to play the events in that same age category.
  • A player enrolled for Singles in a particular age category can also play doubles only in the same age category.
  • The participant must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match.
  • The participant has to report 15 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match time at the Table Tennis registration desk.
  • If the participant fails to report 7 minutes after the match is announced the opponents will get a walkover.
  • Every player must carry his/her school id along with age proof at all times during the tournament.
  • Participants must produce their ID cards as and when required during the tournament.
  • No player will be allowed to play his/her match if they donot have their school ID along with proper age proof.
  • Any participant found over age or with any manipulation in the documents will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • The Table Tennis organizingcommittee has the right to postpone or cancel the tournament in case of unavoidable circumstances. All schools are requested to co-operate in any such case.
  • If any player who cannot take part in the tournament even after giving his/her entry can be replaced with another player on a special request by the respective school at least 1 day before the match starts. This is applicable only in case of doubles.
  • In case of any dispute the decision of the Table tennisorganizingcommittee will be final and binding.
  • No player is allowed to use their mobile phones on court.
  • Participants must be in appropriate uniform for the sport of Table Tennis. Only non-marking / glass sole shoes will be permitted while playing matches.
  • All TTFI Approved Equipment’s will be used in the Tournaments.

  • Entries will be accepted on a first come first basis. However ranked players will be given preference over non-ranked players.
  • First preference will be given to players who have a national or state rank. Players ranking from the period 1st January 2014 up to 25th October 2015 will be considered.
