Sports For All being a platform for school children to participate and compete in multiple sports, will be conducting tournaments for the following 8 sports at its flag-off event in Mumbai:

1. Tennis 2. Squash 3. Badminton 4. Table Tennis
5. Swimming 6. Basketball 7. Volleyball 8. Handball

For the first time an inter-school sports tournament will be officiated and governed by respective authorized bodies for all 8 sports.

The venue for the first tournament - D.Y.Patil Sports Academy, is equipped with state-of-the-art sporting infrastructure, apt to conduct a multi-sporting tournament of this magnitude. Supporting the sporting infrastructure are several other facilities that will be made available to create a sports-conducive and carnival like environment.

A few of the other facilities include:

Restrooms & Changing Rooms First-Aid Care & Physiotherapy
Food, Water & Nutrition Stalls Sports Equipment & Merchandise Stalls
TV Screens and Match Viewing Galleries Participant Warm Up Area
