  • Only 500 Schools will be allowed to participate at the event.
  • Registrations open from 2nd Oct – 15th Nov’15 only.
  • Only students of member schools can participate in any and all SFA events.
  • To become a member school, school authorities are requested to fill out all required details in the registration form and send the physical signed and stamped copies along with your participant entry forms.
  • Schools will have to send cash / demand draft / cheque, in favour of SFA Events Pvt. Ltd. towards an annual registration fee of Rs.5000/-. This fee entails students of the school to participate in tournaments across all 8 sports and under all age categories if they meet the SFA eligibility criteria.
  • There will be no additional fee that schools or students have to pay other than the annual registration fee.
  • In a scenario where no student from a registered school meets our eligibility criteria and is unable to participate in any sport under any category, SFA will refund the annual registration fee.
