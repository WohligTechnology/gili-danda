Kleinetics

Kleinetics is India's premiere Kids Fitness Program backed by scientific training and technology to improve fitness and well being of kids. Through numerous dynamic and static drills, a variety in workouts and an innovative and scientific curriculum, Kleinetics focuses on cardiovascular endurance, strength, speed, agility, reaction time, power, flexibility, balance and co-ordination of the children. Founded by a team of Corporate professionals, Fitness enthusiasts, Doctors & Leading sports coaches - Kleinetics is aimed to bring about a paradigm shift in the way Fitness programs are designed & delivered in India.

   

Michael Phelps Swimming School

Michael Phelps Swimming program is the most accomplished swimming program in the world. The program is designed by Michael Phelps himself along with his childhood coach, Cathy. The vision and goal of this program is to grow the sport across globe for fitness, leisure, water safety and professionally.
