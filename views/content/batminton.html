Badminton at SFA

Badminton at SFA will be conducted under the aegis of the Maharashtra Badminton Association.For the first time an inter-school badminton tournament will be officiated and governed by an authorized body of the sport.

The badminton arena at the venue has three wooden floored courts, suited and calibrated for World-class tournaments.Every match played byparticipants will be captured on video and later showcased on their schools and SFA profile.

All medals across the different categories under badminton, will add to the cumulative medal tally of the school, hence impacting it’s rank.

The SFA Inter-school Badminton tournament will be conducted in the categories mentioned in the table below.

We have a maximum draw capacity as mentioned in the below table. Student entries will be accommodated basis the eligibility criteria set out in Point 3.

  • Tournament is open to all amateur school players from districts of MSDBA / GMBA (Mumbai Suburban District Badminton Association / Greater Mumbai Badminton Association).
  • A knockout tournament will be played across all age groups and event categories for both, Boys and Girls.
  • The matches will be best of 3 games of 15 points each up till the Pre Quarter Finals. The Quarter Finals, Semi Finals and Finals will be best of 3 games of 21 points each.
  • The tournament will be conducted as per the rules & regulations for tournaments as laid down by the BWF and BAI (Badminton World Federation and Badminton Association of India).
  • Each participant can participate in one age category. For example, if a player is participating in the U-11 age category, he is only eligible to play the events in that same category. A player enrolled for Singles in a particular age category can also play doubles only in the same age category.
  • The participant must report at the venue’s main registration desk 45 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match.
  • The participant has to report 15 minutes prior to his/her scheduled match time at the Badminton registration desk.
  • If the participant fails to report 7 minutes after the match is announced the opponents will get a walkover.
  • Every player must carry his/her School ID along with age proof at all times during the tournament.
  • Participant must produce their ID cards as and when required during the tournament.
  • No player will be allowed to play his/her match if they do not have their school ID along with proper age proof.
  • Any participant found over age or with any manipulation in the documents will be disqualified without any kind of prior intimation.
  • Participants must be in appropriate uniform for the sport of Badminton. Only non-marking / gum sole shoes will be permitted while playing matches.
  • No player is allowed to use their mobile phones on court.
  • The Badminton organizing committee has the right to postpone or cancel the tournament in case of unavoidable circumstances. All schools are requested to co-operate in any such case.
  • If any player who cannot take part in the tournament even after giving his/her entry can be replaced with another player on a special request by the respective school at least 1 day before the match starts. This is applicable only in case of doubles.
  • In case of any dispute the decision of the badminton organizing committee will be final and binding.
  • For any kind of further details of the tournament the school Coach / Manager can contact the following mobile no’s:

  • Only students from Sports For All member schools will be eligible to participate.
  • Singles: First preference will be given to district, state and national ranked players. Remaining vacant slots will be allocated on a first come first serve basis.
  • Doubles: Teams will be allocated on a first come first serve basis.
  • Players ranking from the period 1st January 2014 up to 25th October 2015 will be considered.
  • Incomplete details in the entry forms or any manipulations in the entries will not be accepted by the organizing committee.
